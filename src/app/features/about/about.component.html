About Us

Williamsburg Nails is more than just a salon — it's a dream turned into a family tradition. It all began with Nenita, a loving mother who spent years working in nail salons while dreaming of one day owning her own. With patience, passion, and perseverance, she not only mastered her craft but also passed it down to her daughters, Christine, Cindy, and Janette.

In 2008, that dream became a reality with the opening of Nails by Lisa, which later became known as Williamsburg Nails. Together, our family built a place where beauty, connection, and care come first. Every set of nails we create is done with love, and every guest is treated like part of the family.

Whether you're looking for stunning nails, a relaxing escape, or simply a warm and welcoming space to chat — we're here for you. Come visit us at Williamsburg Nails, where heart and artistry meet.
